Why should you join?


Ever wanted to share a persistent chat group with other League players and hit the Rift united under a single, radiant banner? Join the club. By joining our League of Legends club, you can expand your gaming skills and learn so much more about how to communicate throughout the game, strategize, and break your limits. Here at the LoL Club, we let players create their own teams and come together to join a tournament where the winning team will recieve $1000. This tournament is held every year in the summer. Be one of the millions of players who rise above the rest. Whether your a casual or professional league player, we welcome all players.



Requirements


There are not many requirements to join our LoL Club. The only necessary conditions we want our players to meet are:


* Must be 13 years or older
* Must have parents permission if the player is under the age of 18
